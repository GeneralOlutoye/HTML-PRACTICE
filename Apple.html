Biography

Steven paul"Paul" Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was the cofounder, chariman, and CEO of Apple Inc, CEO and larges shareholder of Pixar Animation Studions, and founder, chariman, and CEO of NeXT Inc. Jobs is widely recognised as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak, "creative entrepreneur whose passion for perfection and ferocious drive revolutionized six industiries: personal computers, animated movies, music, phones, tablet computing, and digital publishing."
